SHRI RAMA BHARTI PUBLIC SCHOOL

To meet the needs of the changing world, the management of Shri Ram Gupta Memorial Educational Society has started Shri Rama Bharti Public School at Sainik Nagar, near Sector-06, Bahadurgarh, an exclusive co- educational english medium school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi. Situated just one km. away from the hustle and bustle of the town in a lush green, pollution free environment covering over 3.5 acres of land, the school boasts of having a stupendous main building apart from a beautiful garden and a big playground with track and courts for various sports.
